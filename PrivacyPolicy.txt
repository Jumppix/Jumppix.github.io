Privacy Policy
Last updated: 25.06.2026

Viral Content Publish ("the app") is a personal tool operated by an individual creator to
publish their own short-form videos to their own TikTok account. This policy
explains what data the app handles.

Data the app accesses
- TikTok account information: after you log in with TikTok, the app receives your
  basic profile information (open_id and display name) to confirm it is connected
  to the correct account.
- Authentication tokens: the app stores the OAuth access and refresh tokens issued
  by TikTok so it can act on your behalf. It never receives or stores your password.
- Video content: videos that you choose to upload through the app.

How the data is used
The data is used solely to authenticate the account owner and to upload the owner's
own videos to their own TikTok account. It is not used for any other purpose.

Data sharing
The app does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Account data is
only transmitted to TikTok's official API to perform the actions you request.

Your control
You can revoke the app's access at any time in your TikTok settings under "Manage
app permissions." Revoking access immediately stops the app from acting on your behalf.
